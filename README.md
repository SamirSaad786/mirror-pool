# The Mirror Pool

> What you see in this world and its reflection are not the same.

**The Mirror Pool** is a kitsune tile-swap puzzle game with dual-world mechanics — the solution that works in this world may not be the one the reflection shows you.

## What It Is

A browser-based puzzle game built on Japanese folklore. Players swap tiles to solve puzzles that exist simultaneously in two states — the visible world and its mirror reflection — which don't always match. A nine-tails kitsune progression system tracks your advancement, and a favor/curse mechanic rewards or complicates play based on how you treat the spirit world.

Seven environmental themes shift the visual tone as you progress deeper into the puzzle set.

## Features

- **Dual-world tile mechanics** — visible world and mirror reflection interact independently
- **Nine-tails kitsune progression** — advancement tied to mastery
- **Favor / Curse system** — your choices have spiritual consequences
- **Seven environmental themes** — distinct visual palettes across the puzzle set
- Single-file HTML — no install, no account, no data collection

## Genre & Tone

Puzzle / Japanese Folklore / Dual World  
Mysterious / Folkloric / Strategic
